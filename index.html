Frequently asked question

01. How can Go help me invoice like a company when I don't have one registered?👇
During the onboarding process, we'll ask you to enter into a partnership agreement with us (SoloMove). Don't worry, it's nothing binding. But it does allow you to use our framework to legally invoice clients, collect payments, reimburse business expenses and withdraw earnings to your personal bank account.
02. What kind of businesses are supported by Xolo Go?👈
Xolo Go supports freelancers and solopreneurs offering professional services. Software developers, IT experts, business consultants, marketing and sale gurus, designers, writers, and translators — to name just a few.
03. What kind of clients can I invoice with Xolo Go?👈
Xolo Go empowers you to effortlessly invoice B2B and B2C clients who are registered in any of the EU member states as well as Switzerland, Norway, UK, the USA and Canada.
You can also invoice other Xolo Go users.
Learn more about our Xolo Teams product
04. How do I transfer my earnings to my personal bank account?👈
When you first sign up for Xolo Go we'll ask you to provide the IBAN number for your personal bank account. Once you've got money on your account, you can make withdrawals from the business bank account Xolo provides for you to your personal bank account whenever you'd like. Payments are executed within one business day, so you'll have your money in no time!
Learn more about Withdrawals
05. How difficult is account setup?👈
Surprisingly simple! We'll ask you a few questions about your business to make sure Xolo is a good fit. We'll also verify your identity with the help of our partners at Veriff. You'll need a valid photo ID and a working camera for this step.
Learn more about the identity verification process
06. What's the deal with taxes?👈
Xolo Go only deals with Value Added Tax (VAT). We automatically calculate the VAT owed based on your client's location and add it to each invoice. As much as we'd like to, we aren't yet able to help with your taxes. But we do make tax time simpler in that you can download earnings reports directly from your Xolo Go dashboard. Forward the report to your accountant and watch how quickly you become their favorite client!
07. How much is this going to cost me every month?👈
In exchange for our services we charge a flat-rate fee of 5% whenever you withdraw funds to your personal bank account. We also charge a 5% fee on any business-related purchases that you expense through your Xolo Go dashboard (hello, lower tax bill). But there are no subscriptions, long-term contracts, or unpleasant surprise fees. We only get paid when you get paid.
08. How do I close my account?👈
Xolo Go is a favorite solution for freelancers whose workloads tend to ebb and flow. If you're having a slow period, your Xolo Go account will be waiting for you whenever you decide to start invoicing again (at no extra charge). If you decide Xolo Go isn't right for you, you can close your account right from your account dashboard
